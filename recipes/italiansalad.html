Italian Pasta Salad

Description

Again from my mom but this time it is italian pasta salad. I always can eat all in the big mixing bowl in one sitting! They knew I would do that so they would need to portion quickly before i eat it all! This recipe is different recipe from my mom because I wanted to see different versions of it and This recipe is best ever!

Ingredients

  • Rotini pasta
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Bell pepper
  • Shallot
  • Marinated artichoke hearts
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Cornichons
  • Salami
  • Mozzarella
  • Parsley and Basil
  • Olive oil
  • White wine vinegar
  • Fresh garlic
  • Dried oregano
  • Crushed red pepper flakes and black pepper

Steps

  1. Get ready. Bring a large pot of water to a boil over high heat.
  2. Make the dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together ¼ cup olive oil, 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar, 2 minced garlic cloves, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, a pinch of salt and pepper, and ½ to 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (depending on how spicy you like things). Set aside.
  3. Cook the pasta. Generously salt the boiling water, then add the pasta and cook until al dente according to the package instructions. Drain and add to a large mixing bowl.
  4. Toss the pasta salad. To the bowl with the pasta, add 1 pint halved tomatoes, 1 chopped bell pepper, 1 chopped shallot, 1 cup artichoke hearts, ¼ cup pitted Kalamata olives, 6 chopped pepperoncini, 6-8 ounces sliced salami, 6 ounces mozzarella, and ½ packed cup each of chopped parsley and basil. Toss to combine.
  5. Dress the salad. Pour the dressing over the pasta and toss to coat. Serve immediately, or better yet let it sit for 30 minutes to soak up all the flavor.
